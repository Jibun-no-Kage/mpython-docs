Classic Games
======


Greedy snakes, tetris and other simple and fun classic games were no stranger to the 80s and 90s. They are simple gameplay and durable popular mobile games.
Earlier at their time, there were no dazzling online games, no Android, Apple, and no mobile games.

Lets make use of the mPython Board and Python programming to reproduce those classic games.
